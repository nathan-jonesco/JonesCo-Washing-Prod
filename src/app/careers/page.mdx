# Join Our Team at JonesCo Window and Pressure Washing

At **JonesCo Window and Pressure Washing**, we pride ourselves on being a family-owned and community-driven business. Our success is built on a foundation of hard work, integrity, and a commitment to excellence in every service we offer. If you share these values and are passionate about making a difference in your community, we invite you to join our team.

## Current Job Opening: Exterior Maintenance Technician

We are currently seeking a dedicated and skilled **Exterior Maintenance Technician** to join our growing team. This role is essential to the high-quality service we provide to our customers, ensuring that every project is completed with precision and care.

### Job Responsibilities

- Perform a variety of exterior maintenance tasks, including window cleaning, pressure washing, gutter cleaning, and more.
- Ensure all work is completed to the highest standards of quality and safety.
- Communicate effectively with customers to understand their needs and provide exceptional service.
- Maintain and operate company equipment in a responsible and efficient manner.
- Assist with other tasks as needed to support the team and business.

### Qualifications

- Experience in exterior home maintenance, window cleaning, or pressure washing is preferred but not required.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
- Strong attention to detail and commitment to quality.
- Excellent communication and customer service skills.
- Physical ability to perform manual labor, including lifting and working at heights.
- Valid driver's license and reliable transportation.

### What We Offer

- Competitive pay and benefits.
- A supportive and family-oriented work environment.
- Opportunities for growth and advancement within the company.
- The chance to be part of a business that values its employees and the community it serves.

## How to Apply

If you are interested in joining the JonesCo family as an Exterior Maintenance Technician, please submit your resume and a brief cover letter to careers@jonescowashing.com explaining why you would be a great fit for our team. We look forward to hearing from you!
